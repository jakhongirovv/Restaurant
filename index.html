Whitefish salad is a salad of smoked freshwater whitefish and mayonnaise.
Whitefish salad is a staple fare of Ashkenazi Jewish American cuisine, often
found at appetizing stores and Jewish delicatessens.Whitefish salad originated
in North America among Ashkenazi Jewish immigrants. Ashkenazi Jews discovered
that the freshwater whitefish, found in the Great Lakes, was similar to freshwater
whitefish found in Europe, and soon smoked freshwater whitefish became a
staple of Ashkenazi Jewish appetizing stores and delicatessens
and became an iconic example of Jewish American cuisine.
